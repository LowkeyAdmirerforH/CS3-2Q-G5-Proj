Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo and the Fertilizer Fund Scam

Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo, the former president who held office from 2001 to 2010, was accused of corruption both during and after her term. The Fertilizer Fund Scam, which reportedly involved the misappropriation of PHP 728 million in government funds meant to assist underprivileged farmers, was one of the most controversial incidents associated with her administration.

The scandal highlighted how public funds meant for crucial agricultural aid were allegedly redirected to support political campaigns.
